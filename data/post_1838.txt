      哪一學年度修課：105-1 五234  @原分所R311

      不可逆熱力學及其應用
      Irreversible Thermodynamics and its Application in Spin Caloritronics

      ψ 授課教師 (若為多人合授請寫開課教師，以方便收錄)

         楊大衍(中研院原分所)，張嘉升教授是掛名

      λ 開課系所與授課對象 (是否為必修或通識課 / 內容是否與某些背景相關) 

         理學院  物理學研究所  222 D3410 博士班選修，但最後都是碩班來修

         滿五位同學開課，最後都只有三位在上，中央物理所碩班和台大機械所碩班

      δ 課程大概內容

        Week1:  Introduction: Overview and History about Thermodynamics
        Week2:  Heat, Work, Internal Energy, Temperature
        Week3:  Ideal Gas, Specific heat and Carnot Engine
        Week4:  Entropy and Thermodynamic Potentials
        Week5:  Maxwell Relations, Gibbs-Helmholtz Equation
        Week6:  Heat Engines (Rankine, Otto, Diesel, Brayton)
        Week7:  Solution Thermodynamics (Chemical Potential, Activity)
        Week8:  Concept of Flux: Electrical Flux and Vector Analysis
        Week9:  Fluid Mechanics: Stress Tensor and Continuity Equation
        Week10: Fluid Mechanics: Navier–Stokes Equations
        Week11: --No Class--
        Week12: Irreversible Process, Flux-Force and Phenomenological Equation
        Week13: Fluctuation-Dissipation Theory, Balance Equations
        Week14: Balance Equations, Thermoelectricity and its Effects 
        Week15: General Conductivity Matrix and Spin Caloritronics
        Week16: Statistical Thermodynamics: S = klnΩ, Partition Function
        Week17: Statistical Thermodynamics: Ensemble and Brownian Motion
        Week18: --No Class--

        上色約3~4周部分為不可逆熱力學獨有，而其他部分可分散於其他課程所見
